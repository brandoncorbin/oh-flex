4 Material Colors #Colors

Oh-Flex contains all of the colors from the Material Design spec (https://material.io/guidelines/style/color.html) to apply a fill add f-bg-COLORNAME, to apply a color to text or an icon use f-t-COLORNAME

<div f-bg-blue-700>I'm filled with Blue</div>
<div f-t-blue-500>I'm Blue Text</div>
